# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want participation in Personal Library to be useful, respectful, and safe. Be considerate,
assume good intent while addressing impact, welcome people with different experience levels, and
keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct,
deliberate intimidation, publishing another person's private information, or sustained disruption
of project spaces.

## Scope and enforcement

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and other spaces
where someone represents the project. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close interactions,
or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately through a contact method listed on the repository owner's GitHub
profile. Do not use a public issue for sensitive reports. Maintainers will review reports in good
faith, protect reporter privacy where practical, and avoid conflicts of interest.
